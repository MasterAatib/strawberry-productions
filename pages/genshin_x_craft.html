Description

Genshin X Craft is a Minecraft Bedrock addon inspired by Genshin Impact. It introduces elemental weapons, mobs, and mechanics designed to feel close to the original game while remaining balanced for survival gameplay.

Explore a fantasy-style experience where elements matter, combat feels dynamic, and your Minecraft world becomes infused with Genshin energy.

Features

  • Custom Genshin-inspired weapons
  • Elemental effects (Pyro, Cryo, Electro, etc.)
  • New mobs and bosses
  • Survival-friendly balance
  • Bedrock Edition support
