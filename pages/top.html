Description

Tournament of Power is a meticulously crafted Minecraft Bedrock map based on the legendary arc from Dragon Ball Super. Fight across a massive floating arena suspended in the void, surrounded by the energy of the World of Void.

Choose your fighter, gather your team, and engage in explosive PvP battles or cooperative survival challenges. The map captures the spirit of the anime with custom structures, hidden secrets, and an atmosphere charged with ki.

Features

  • Fully detailed Tournament of Power arena
  • Multiple iconic locations from the arc
  • Custom PvP areas with ring-out mechanics
  • Hidden Dragon Balls and Easter eggs
  • Works in both Survival and Creative modes
  • Optimized for multiplayer battles (up to 10 players)
